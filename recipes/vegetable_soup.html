Creamy vegetable soup

Description

This simple soup recipe delivers all year round. It can be varied by using different types of vegetables. Also, vegans can replace the cream with cashew cream.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Hot red chilli, deseeded and chopped
  • Onion, roughly chopped
  • Garlic, two peeled cloves
  • Courgette, washed and roughly chopped
  • Carrot, peeled and chopped
  • Potato, peeled and roughly chopped
  • Vegetable stock, about 500ml
  • Parsley, chopped
  • Cream or double cream
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a big pot, heat two tablespoons of olive oil. Add the chilli and onion, and fry for about 10 minutes until the onion is soft and translucent. Add the garlic and fry for another couple of minutes.
  2. Reduce the heat and add the potato, courgette, carrot, and vegetable stock. Simmer until the vegetables are done. Take off the heat and add the chopped parsley and cream.
  3. Use a hand-held blender to puree the mix and turn it into a creamy soup. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with crusty bread.
